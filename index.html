Knowledge-Augmented Machine Learning Group

Code Publications

The Knowledge-Augmented Machine Learning Group (KAMeL) at the University of São Paulo is directed by Prof. Denis Mauá. The research group aims at designing algorithms for probabilistic and logical reasoning, combining symbolic knowledge representation together with statistical learning for neuro-symbolic research. The KAMeL Group is primarily interested in the following research topics:

  • Probabilistic Graphical Models
  • Probabilistic Circuits
  • Probabilistic Programming
  • Statistical Relational Learning
  • Planning under Uncertainty
  • Imprecise Probability
