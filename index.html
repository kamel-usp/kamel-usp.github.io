This is the website of the Knowledge-Augmented Machine Learning Group (KAMeL) at the University of São Paulo.

About

The group investigates computational aspects of knowledge-rich approaches to machine learning, with a focus on techniques that combine logic and probabilistic reasoning. The group is related to the Laboratory of Formal Methods and Artificial Intelligence (LIAMF) of the Institute of Mathematics and Statistics, and and to the Center of Artificial Intelligence of USP (C4AI).
