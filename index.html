This is the website of the Knowledge-Augmented Machine Learning Group (KAMeL) at the University of São Paulo.

About

The group investigates computational aspects of probabilistic and logical reasoning, with a focus on logic-inspired methods for Machine Learning. The group is part of the Laboratory of Formal Methods and Artificial Intelligence (LIAMF) of the Institue of Mathematics and Statistics, and related to the Center of Artificial Intelligence of USP.
